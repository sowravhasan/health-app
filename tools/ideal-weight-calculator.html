Calculation Methods

Robinson Formula (1983)

Most commonly used in clinical practice. Based on height with gender-specific adjustments.

Miller Formula (1983)

Similar to Robinson but with slightly different coefficients for more accurate results.

Devine Formula (1974)

Original formula used for drug dosage calculations, now adapted for ideal weight.

Hamwi Formula (1964)

Quick estimation method, widely used in nutrition and dietetics.
